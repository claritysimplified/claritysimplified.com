About This Project

Built to help you understand the bill

This site exists to raise public awareness of the CLARITY Act of 2025 (H.R. 3633) by translating it into plain English.

We are not part of this process

This website is an independent, volunteer educational project. The people who built it are not affiliated with Congress, any bill sponsor, any government agency, any campaign, or any company, and we are not involved in drafting, lobbying for, funding, or advancing this legislation in any way.

Our only goal is education: to help everyday people read and understand the CLARITY Act in plain English so they can form their own opinions. Nothing here is legal, financial, or political advice, and it is not an endorsement. For the authoritative text, always consult the official bill on Congress.gov.
